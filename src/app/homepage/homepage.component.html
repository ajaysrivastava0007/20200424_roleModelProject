A Tribute To Our Missile-Man of India

Dr A.P.J Abdul Kalam






Born 15th October 1931
Rameshwaram, Madras
Died 27th July 2015 (aged 83)
Profession Aerospace Scientist
Author
Awards Padma Bhushan(1981)
Padma Vibhushan(1990)
Bharat Ratna(1997)
HooverMedal(2009)

Website abdulkalam.com

Some of his Great Lines are:


  1. Never stop fighting until you arrive at your destined place - that is, the unique you. Have an aim in life, continuously acquire knowledge, work hard, and have perseverance to realise the great life.

  2. Educationists should build the capacities of the spirit of inquiry, creativity, entrepreneurial and moral leadership among students and become their role model.

  3. It means, people who are in high and responsible positions, if they go against righteousness, righteousness itself will get transformed into a destroyer.

  4. Never stop fighting until you arrive at your destined place - that is, the unique you. Have an aim in life, continuously acquire knowledge, work hard, and have perseverance to realise the great life.

  5. Climbing to the top demands strength, whether it is to the top of Mount Everest or to the top of your career.
